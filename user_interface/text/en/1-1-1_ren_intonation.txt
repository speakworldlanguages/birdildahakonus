All spoken languages on Earth feature tonality which helps one better express his or her mood and enables us to emphasize that which we think is more important. However when speaking Renmen language (a.k.a. Chinese) tonality does a lot more; <strong>it gives words totally different meanings</strong>. Remember that you must pay attention to the intonation (raising or lowering the pitch of your voice) in every syllable until you get used to this <span style="white-space:nowrap;">“ nonstop singing ”.</span>
