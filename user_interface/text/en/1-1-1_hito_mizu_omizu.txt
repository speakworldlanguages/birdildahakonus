In Hito language (a.k.a. Japanese) names of things that are associated with human survival or public welfare can take the honorific prefix <span style="white-space:nowrap;">[o-].</span> At the end of this lesson, there will be a speaking test where you must talk to your device microphone. During the test you may say it either with or without the <span style="white-space:nowrap;">[o-] prefix;</span> the app will accept both.
