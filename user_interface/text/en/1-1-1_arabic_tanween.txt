In standard Arabic, adding the <span style="white-space: nowrap;">[-n] sound</span> at the end of nouns is called "Tanween" or "Nunation". Tanween does not change the meaning of a word. Once you have seen enough many examples you will be able to know when to and when not to use tanween (we recommend letting it happen over time). Now you may say any of the two forms into your device microphone; the app will accept both.
